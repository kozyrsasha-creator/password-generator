Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2025

1) Our approach

We aim to run GetPassnow without tracking cookies. The password generator works entirely in your browser and does not depend on cookies for its main functionality.

2) Preferences and local storage

If you use optional features in the future (for example, theme selection or language preference), your browser may store these settings in local storage. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3) Third-party assets

Static resources such as Bootstrap or icons are loaded from trusted CDNs. These providers may keep minimal operational logs or set strictly necessary cookies under their own policies. We do not control or access third-party cookies.

4) How to control cookies

You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings. Refer to your browser’s documentation for instructions. Blocking cookies will not affect the generator’s ability to work properly.

5) Updates

We may revise this cookie policy to reflect future changes to the site or its dependencies. The “Last updated” date will be adjusted accordingly.
