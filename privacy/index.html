Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2025

1) What this policy covers

GetPassnow is a browser-based password generator. We design it to work fully on your device so your generated passwords never leave your browser.

2) Data we do not collect

  • We do not store, transmit, or log generated passwords.
  • We do not require accounts or personal details to use the tool.
  • We do not use third-party analytics by default.

3) Data we may process

When you load the website, standard web server logs may record your IP address and user agent for short-term security and reliability (e.g., mitigating abuse). These logs are automatically rotated and are not linked to any profile.

4) Cookies and local storage

By default, we do not set any tracking cookies. Your password is generated in memory. If you enable future features (e.g., theme), your browser may store preferences locally; those never leave your device.

5) Third-party infrastructure

We host the static site on a reputable provider. Static assets (like CSS) may be served from a CDN. Those providers may collect minimal operational logs as described in their policies.

6) Security

We follow the principle of data minimization. The generator uses modern browser cryptography APIs to produce randomness locally. Always keep your device updated and avoid sharing passwords over insecure channels.

7) Your rights

Since we do not maintain user accounts or store generated data, there is nothing to access or delete regarding your passwords. For general questions, see the FAQ on the home page.

8) Changes

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
